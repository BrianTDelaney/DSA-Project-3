Hollow Knight is a 2D side-scrolling Metroidvania. The player controls a silent insectoid protagonist called "the Knight" who explores an underground fallen kingdom called Hallownest. The Knight can strike enemies with a sword-like weapon called a Nail and can learn spells that allow for long-range attacks.
